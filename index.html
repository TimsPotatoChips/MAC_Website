Why M.A.C?

Coding is not just something you learn, it’s something you live! We give students and adults from all walks of life a space to put their knowledge into action and create real projects that matter. Too many talented people never get the chance to use their skills beyond lessons and theory. We make sure that changes by opening the door for you to explore, build, and grow, no matter your age, skill level, or background.

Weekly interactive seminars with personalized 1-on-1 mentorship from talented experts across the globe. We don’t just teach coding, we help you build real projects, sharpen problem-solving skills, and prepare for the future.

Gain hands-on coding experience, access exclusive learning resources, connect with a global community, and enjoy mentorship that’s tailored to your goals.

From exclusive recognition badges to priority access to advanced workshops, we celebrate every milestone you achieve, because your growth deserves to be seen.

Earn industry-recognized certificates that showcase your skills and dedication. Each certificate is a badge of achievement you can proudly display on your portfolio or LinkedIn profile.

Our Mission

M.A.C provides opportunities to learn computer science while gaining real-world experience that prepares you for future careers.
